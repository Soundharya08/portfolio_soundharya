SMART AGRICULTURAL SYSTEM FOR VERTICAL GARDENING

Smart agricultural systems for vertical gardening are unique because they allow farmers to efficiently use space by stacking crops vertically.
By using sensors and actuators, these systems can optimize the use of space even further and grow crops closer together without sacrificing yield or plant health. Vertical gardening systems create a controlled environment for crops to grow in, which can lead to higher yields and healthier plants.

The main objective is it automatically swiching of pump,Reduction in usage of water ,Reduction in labour and wireless communication soil moisture sensor,water level sensor are the different sensors used to sense the moisture level
